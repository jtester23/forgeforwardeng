# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Pharmaceutical, biotechnology, and life science teams looking for GxP consulting support across site startup, validation, computerized systems, equipment qualification, GMP programs, and regulatory readiness.

## Product Purpose

Forge Forward Engineering presents a focused consulting practice that helps clients move facilities, systems, equipment, and programs through startup and routine operation with stronger compliance posture. Success means a visitor quickly understands the firm's life science specialization, sees the service areas, trusts the founder-led expertise, and contacts the firm with a relevant consulting inquiry.

## Brand Personality

Precise, durable, and steady. The brand should feel like a trusted engineering partner for regulated environments: calm under scrutiny, technically fluent, and practical rather than flashy.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS startup polish, playful biotech illustration, templated icon-card grids, luxury editorial styling, and vague innovation language that weakens the seriousness of GxP work.

## Design Principles

1. Lead with regulatory confidence: every section should reinforce readiness, control, and follow-through.
2. Make services scannable: visitors should understand the consulting areas without digging through long pages.
3. Show founder-led accountability: the team section should feel personal but still professional.
4. Keep the surface lean: use the existing content, stronger hierarchy, and better pacing rather than bloated marketing copy.
5. Preserve contact intent: the page should always make it easy to start a consultation conversation.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA basics: strong contrast, visible focus states, semantic landmarks, useful labels, reduced-motion support, and responsive layouts that keep forms usable on mobile.
